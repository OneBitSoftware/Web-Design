The space

In the historic quarter of Santo Spirito, on the left bank of the river Arno, studio apartment is perfect for those travelling alone or as a couple. To walk between Santo Spirito, Ponte Vecchio and Boboli Gardens is a magical experience.

On the third floor of a typical Florentine building, the apartment consists of an entrance with wardrobes and loft with double bed, equipped kitchen and bathroom with shower.
